Questions

The honest FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. The Trend Friend publishes systematic observations — the output of one transparent trend rule applied identically to every asset — plus editorial commentary. It never gives personalised recommendations, entries, exits or position sizes. What you do with the information is your decision and your risk.

What exactly is the signal?

A disciplined EMA-based trend test on daily closes, applied the same way to every asset. Green means the uptrend test passes, red means the downtrend test passes, neutral means neither. We'll publish the full methodology at launch — transparency is the point.

1,200+ at launch: global large-cap stocks (US, UK, Europe, Asia), ~100 cryptocurrencies, 25 major indices, 11 forex pairs and 10 commodities — with recent IPOs added automatically as they season.
